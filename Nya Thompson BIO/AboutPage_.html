Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY, Nya Thompson has always been a lover of media. Stemming from her early days in high school where she worked alongside DCTV to compose a documentary, Nya went on to receive her Bachelor’s Degree in Broadcast Journalism from Quinnipiac University.  As an undergraduate, Nya immediately immersed herself in the campus television programs QNN News and #That, focusing both on campus and town related news as well as pop culture. Nya has obtained a series of positions throughout the media world. Upon graduating from undergrad, she dived right on the scene interning at various digital publications including Tha. L Spot., and PYNK Magazine. With a love of culture and community, Nya teamed up with Brooklyn-based cultural lifestyle brand, CaribBEING. While working as a Digital Marketing and Event Specialist for the brand she worked to build social media campaigns celebrating Caribbean art, film, and culture. She also assisted in the execution of two of the brands major events, the Heritaage Film Series at the Brooklyn Musuem and a Tenement Talk at the Tenement Musuem. Nya believes in the importance of media and the influence it has on todays society. More than anything she loves to apply her appplied knowledge of the craft to everything that she does. Although Nya is currently a contributing writer to online publication Brooklyn Buttah, she is always open to ventures throuhout the world of media.
